About

  • June 06, 2020

    Our Humble Beginnings

    HPD was founded on June 6th, 2020, by our visionary founder, Joseph Ibrahim, amidst the challenges of the, COVID-19 pandemic. What began as humble beginnings has since evolved into a dynamic company with a diverse range of services.

  • March 2023

    An Agency is Born

    Since March 2023, our design, branding, and printing services have embraced a comprehensive marketing and branding strategy that emphasizes the unique value proposition we offer to our clients. By focusing on targeted audience analysis and refining our messaging, we’ve been able to effectively communicate the quality, creativity, and reliability of our services. Our approach has been to highlight the seamless integration of design aesthetics with functional branding solutions, ensuring that each project we undertake not only meets but exceeds client expectations. This client-centric strategy has resulted in increased brand visibility and a stronger market presence, positioning us as a leading choice for businesses seeking to make a lasting impression through their visual identity

  • Today 2024

    Transition to Full Service

    Today, HPD boasts a talented team of four individuals, each bringing their unique skills and expertise to the table. Brenda Jebet serves as our dedicated Executive Assistant, ensuring smooth operations and communication. Elizabeth Achieng' leads our creative endeavors, infusing every project with innovation and flair. John Ochieng lends his design expertise to our projects, while Joseph Ibrahim spearheads our creative direction, guiding our vision and strategy.

  • April 2024

    Our Trajectory Expansion

    As we continue to grow, HPD is on a trajectory of expansion, taking on both remote and physical jobs with equal enthusiasm and dedication. With our blend of creativity, professionalism, and adaptability, we are committed to delivering exceptional results for our clients, no matter the challenge.
